Privacy & Compliance

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Protection

DIVEXA Global follows strict international data protection standards. Any center or candidate data shared via Nomee Solutions is encrypted and never sold to third parties.

2. Zero Upfront Fee Policy

Our recruitment and B2B campaign onboarding is 100% free. We strictly prohibit any employee or partner from asking for "Security Deposits" or "Processing Fees".

3. Direct Buyer Transparency

We only facilitate direct buyer-to-center connectivity. All CRM data and buffer logs are the property of the respective campaign buyers.

4. Global Operations

Our operations in Dubai, Bangkok, and India comply with local labor laws. We prioritize a peaceful and professional natured environment for all staff.
